Ming for iOS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Overview

Ming is a Chinese literacy app for heritage speakers learning to read Chinese. This policy explains the information Ming uses to provide sign-in, progress sync, review, saved cards, notes, settings, and purchase access.

  • Ming does not sell personal data.
  • Ming does not use advertising trackers.
  • Ming does not use production analytics in v1.
  • Ming has no ads, no subscription, and no AI chat.

Information Ming Collects

When you sign in, Ming collects account identifiers from Apple Sign in or Google Sign-In as needed to create and manage your account.

Ming stores learning data needed for app functionality, including saved cards, notes, review history, lesson progress, settings, account identifiers, and purchase access state.

Progress Sync

If you are signed in, Ming may sync your progress through Firebase Auth and Firebase Firestore so your learning state can be restored and kept consistent across sessions.

Purchases

Paid lesson packs use Apple StoreKit and Apple In-App Purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple. Ming may store purchase access state so the app can unlock content you have paid for and help restore access when available.

Account Deletion

Account deletion is available inside the app at Profile > Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your Ming account and associated learning data from Ming's active app systems, subject to any backup, security, transaction, or legal retention needs.
