Support

Need help with Ming? Email synai.claude@gmail.com.

Account and Login Help

Ming supports Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple. If you cannot access your account, include the sign-in method you used and a brief description of what happened when you email support.

Purchase and Restore Purchase Help

Paid lesson packs are processed by Apple through In-App Purchase. If restore purchase is available in your version of the app, use it to recover access to lesson packs purchased with the same Apple ID.

Progress Sync Help

If your saved cards, notes, review history, lesson progress, or settings do not appear as expected, confirm that you are signed in with the same Apple or Google account. Progress may sync through Firebase/Firestore when you are signed in.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account inside the app at Profile > Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. For questions about deletion or account access, email support.
